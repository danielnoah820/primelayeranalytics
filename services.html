What I do

Core data services

I work with small and medium businesses, e-commerce brands, consultants, logistics operators, and finance-driven companies who want clearer visibility on their numbers. If you're dealing with messy spreadsheets, manual reporting, scattered data sources, or a lack of understandable KPIs, I help you build clean, automated, and actionable data systems that support smarter decisions and stronger performance.

Data Cleaning & Preparation

I transform messy, inconsistent, or incomplete datasets into clean, structured, analysis-ready information. This includes removing duplicates, fixing errors, standardizing formats, merging datasets, defining consistent metrics, and turning your data into a reliable asset that supports dashboards and reports.

Dashboards & KPI Design

I build modern, intuitive dashboards in Power BI, Tableau, or Excel, tailored to the exact KPIs that matter for your business. These dashboards give you instant visibility on revenue, costs, operations, sales performance, customer behaviour, and any indicators you need to track progress and make the right decisions at the right time.

Business Insights & Analysis

Beyond dashboards, I analyze trends, patterns, and performance drivers in your data to uncover what is working and what isn’t. I help you understand where your growth comes from, where money is lost, which products or clients generate the most value, and how to optimize your operations with data-driven recommendations.

Automation & Reporting Tools

I create automated reporting pipelines using Excel models, Power BI refreshes, or Python scripts. These systems eliminate repetitive manual work and generate updated dashboards or reports automatically — saving you time, reducing human error, and ensuring your team always works with up-to-date information.

Custom Data Solutions

If your needs don’t fit into a standard service, I design fully customized data solutions for your workflow: forecasting models, financial analysis tools, operational monitoring systems, CRM data structuring, or tailored decision-support tools. Your problem becomes a structured, automated, precise data solution.
